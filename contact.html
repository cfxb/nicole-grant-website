Get in touch

Contact

A good next step is a free 15-minute phone consultation. It's a chance to talk about what's going on for you, what you're looking for, what you'd get out of therapy, cost, and the options for working together. There's no obligation to book anything further.

How to book

Email with your name and your general availability.

Please don't include sensitive personal details in this first email. I usually reply within three days.

The consultation itself is a phone call, about 15 minutes, at no charge.

Good to know

  • Sessions are online only, across BC, and 50 minutes long.
  • Daytime, evening, and weekend appointments are available, including after-school times for younger clients.
  • Fees are discussed during the free consultation.
  • I'm a CVAP-approved counsellor and don't charge a CVAP top-up.

You can also view my listing with the BC Association of Clinical Counsellors.
